FIRE EXTINGUISHING ROBO

The Fire Extinguishing Robot is an autonomous system designed to detect and extinguish fires, aimed at addressing fire emergencies efficiently. This robot utilizes a microcontroller to manage various sensors and control mechanisms, ensuring prompt and accurate fire detection and response.

SOLAR BAG MONITORING

The Solar Bag Monitoring System is an innovative project that utilizes solar energy to power a portable monitoring solution for various applications such as charging electronic devices and tracking environmental conditions. This system is integrated into a regular backpack, making it both functional and sustainable for travelers, hikers, and outdoor enthusiasts.
